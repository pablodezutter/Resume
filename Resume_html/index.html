The Story of Me:

A great deal of my life I worked with kids. I started my career as a teacher. Not just a teacher, one for toddlers. Yeah, that one. But after a year it became clear that jobs are being fought for and
toddlers are no easy audience. So, I moved on. I also really moved with my girlfriend in an rented apartment and found an other job. Daycare. Kids. Fun. Twothousandandfive.
...[Five years later]...
Daycare. Kids. Crowded. Noise. So, back on the search as a teacher.
It all took a turn when I began classes in the sales world as a commercial assistant. Still didn't do the trick to get a proper job in sales.
Self-confidence is good and in sales you need lots of it. But that was a problem. So I did call-agency. Cold calling, selling off products... After a year of hard work and coaching - they had so much patience! - I realized:... I am not really a pure salesman.

Skills:expertise or talent

But what am I then? Always a hard subject to talk about. I've got skills. Off course, I have, everyone has. But what should I mention? Well, I am walking on earth for 38 years and in that time I've mastered:

Creating: as a kid, student and teacher. Materials: all sorts, even languages. Problem-solving: if I dig for a solution I like it to be long-lasting. Think: prefer to think out of the box, although I like been told what to do, I still like to think for myself. Diapering: yeah well, what can I say...
